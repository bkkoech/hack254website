What is Hack254 ?

Hack254 is a 12 (or 24) hour hackathon for college students and tech community members between the ages of 18 and 26. Participants of this hackathon will get to work on projects in teams to provide tech solutions in areas that interest them.
The event is organised by innovators for developers, coders and engineers.


Sign Up Now!

Our objectives

Application

The objective of this event is to encourage developers to apply their coding skills learnt in classrooms and elsewhere to areas that interest them and social impact.

Collaboration

This event is designed to encourage participants to learn new concepts, programming languages and meet other tech enthusiasts.

Awareness

This event seeks to raise awareness on homegrown solutions and showcase them to enablers for betterment of the Kenya solution landscape.

What to expect at the event

Prizes

Winners will be awarded: The team selected for best overall will receive $250 and the fan favorite team will receive $150. Other prizes to be given out by our partner companies will be announced at the event.

Shirts

All participant will get a free shirt with the Hack254 logo.

Food and transport

We will provide transport to and from the hackathon venue and food at the venue.

WiFi

Fast internet access will be provided courtesy of our partner the Mpesa Foundation.

Tech community

This event welcomes participants from all tech backgrounds to join in on the fun of inventing in just 12(or 24) hours.

Mentors

We have mentors from various technical background.

Eligibility

Participants must meet the following requirements:

  • Must be between the ages of 18 and 25.
  • Must be able to commit to attending the entire event.
  • Must be willing to work with teams on projects.

Hackathon Rules:

In order to be considered for awards participants must abide by the hackathon rules outlined by Hack254.
If any team is found in violation of of the rules they will be disqualified. For a list of the rules and regulations click below to view the participants' brochure.
